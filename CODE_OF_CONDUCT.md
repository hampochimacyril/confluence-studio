# Code Of Conduct

Confluence Studio is a HackSimBuild project meant to welcome contributors from
different disciplines and experience levels. We value clear communication,
respectful disagreement, and practical collaboration.

## Expected Behavior

- Be kind and specific in feedback.
- Assume good intent, especially across disciplines.
- Explain acronyms and domain assumptions when they may not be obvious.
- Credit people for ideas, data, code, design work, and presentation work.
- Keep discussions focused on improving the project and helping users.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, intimidation, or discriminatory language.
- Dismissing non-coding contributions as less valuable.
- Publicly shaming people for mistakes or unfamiliarity with tools.
- Publishing private data, private paths, or private contact information without consent.

## Reporting

For the hackathon, report concerns to the project lead or a HackSimBuild mentor.
For a public repository, open a private channel with the maintainers once a
maintainer contact is listed in the README.
